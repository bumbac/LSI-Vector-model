Title: Our Favourites: Three Buildings Made of Brick in Germany

Text: Thinking Around the Corner: Residential Building by Hehnpohl Architektur in Münster  The residential building by Hehnpohl Architektur in Münster's Buddenstraße responds to the urban context in a variety of ways. The façade is as multifaceted as the interior spaces. (As multifaceted as the facade is, as diversely structured are the interiors.) The result is a varied sequence of rooms that is supplied with ample daylight through the roof.  Client: privat Architecture: hehnpohl architektur Structural Design, Thermal and Sound Insulation: Wiening Ingenieurgemeinschaft, Warendorf Location: Buddenstraße 10, 48143 Münster (DE)
