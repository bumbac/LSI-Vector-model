Title: The Office is Everywhere. Now What? – Editorial 3.2021

Text: Never before have living and working come so spatially close to each other as they do today. What does this mean for architecture? What kinds of layouts and typologies are suited to both living and working? Will this novel alliance impact urban planning in the future? Our Editorial-Podcast with key questions for our March issue “Live and Work”. Our Concept-issue Detail 3.2021 documents recent examples that combine both, living and working.
