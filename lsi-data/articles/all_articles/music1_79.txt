Title: Grammys 2017: The Full Nominations List

Text: The 59th Annual Grammy Awards will be held February 12 at the Staples Center in Los Angeles and broadcast live on CBS. James Corden will host, replacing LL Cool J, who hosted the last five ceremonies. Today, the Recording Academy has announced the 2017 Grammy nominees. Beyoncé, Adele, Drake, Rihanna, Chance the Rapper, Kanye West, and more are all up for multiple awards. Find highlights from the list of nominations below, and the full list here.
