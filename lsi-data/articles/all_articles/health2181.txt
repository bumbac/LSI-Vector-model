Title: Coconut Chicken Cutlets With Spring Pea Salad Recipe

You may be able to find more information about this and similar content on their web site. Directions In a large bowl, whisk together sesame oil, ginger,11/2 Tbsp lime juice, and1/2 tsp salt. Add snap peas, snow peas, and scallions, and toss to combine. Cut each chicken breast horizontally in half to make 4 thin cutlets, then pound to an even thickness. Heat olive oil in a large skillet on medium-high. Season chicken with ½ tsp each salt and pepper and cook until golden brown,2 minutes per side. Reduce heat to low and add coconut milk. Simmer until chicken is just cooked through and sauce has thickened, 1 to 2 minutes. Transfer chicken to plates. Add remaining 1½ Tbsp lime juice to the skillet, scraping up any browned bits. Spoon sauce over chicken and sprinkle with lime zest. Fold cilantro into pea mixture and serve on top of chicken. Drizzle with sesame oil, if desired.
