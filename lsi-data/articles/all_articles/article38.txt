Title: India could be back as the world's fastest growing economy this year

Text: London (CNN Business) After plunging into recession for the first time in nearly a quarter of a century, India's economy should come roaring back to life in 2021. India's gross domestic product is projected to expand by 12.6% during the country's fiscal year starting in April, according to a forecast released Tuesday by the Organization for Economic Cooperation and Development. The OECD also unveiled major upgrades to its global outlook on Tuesday, saying that "economic prospects have improved markedly in recent months" thanks to the deployment of coronavirus vaccines and additional stimulus announcements.
